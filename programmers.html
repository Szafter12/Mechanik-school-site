programming

Scored subjects: Polish, mathematics, computer science, English

Extended subjects: mathematics, English

Duration: 5 years

qualifications

INF.03. Creating and administering websites, internet applications and databases

INF.04. Design, programming and testing of applications

employment

The creation of the programmer technician program at the Technical and General Education School Complex in Limanowa is a response to the demand of the labor market. The school holds talks with employers and monitors the shortage of

employees in particular industries. In the IT sector and in institutions related to business, information processing and related fields, the demand for programmers is very high. Creating a branch and qualifying future graduates will help solve the problem of lack of people who can effectively program and test software.

programmers perspective

Students who will start studying in the above-mentioned field from September will gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in creating programs, web applications, mobile applications, database and website design. Students will learn to write code in popular software languages such as C#, PHP, Java, HTML, Java Script, etc.

In addition, the student will learn various technologies related to programming and the IT industry. Completing a school as a programmer technician will enable graduates to start a career in the labor market, open a business or continue their studies at university.
